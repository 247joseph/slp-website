Overview

Clear Strategy for Civil Disputes.

Civil disputes turn on documents, pleadings and procedure as much as on the merits. We prepare meticulously, plead precisely and advise candidly on whether to fight or settle.

We act for individuals and businesses in money recovery, contractual disputes, specific performance, injunctions and declaratory suits before civil courts across Kerala and the High Court.
