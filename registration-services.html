Overview

Precise Drafting. Smooth Registration.

A deed is only as strong as its drafting and registration. Errors in description, stamp duty or execution can render a transfer vulnerable for years. Our team handles the entire process end to end — at the Sub-Registrar's office and beyond.

We advise on the correct instrument, compute stamp duty and registration fees accurately, prepare the document, and coordinate execution and registration so your transaction is legally sound and properly recorded.
